## 💡 Important Tips for Conversion (PPO)

1.  **Selection Criteria:** For these roles, companies value **learnability** and **reliability** over deep experience. Since you have a 9.85 CGPA, you already clear the "Reliability" bar.
2.  **Tech Focus:** Most Trainee programs in MNCs (Walmart/Target) will involve a rotation. Be ready to show versatility in your tech stack (Node.js/Java/Python).
3.  **Certifications:** While not mandatory, having a **Cloud Certification (AWS/GCP)** or a strong **GitHub portfolio** of your Darwinbox-level projects is a massive differentiator.
4.  **Performance Feedback:** In internship-to-FTE roles, the conversion is 80% based on your final month's project delivery and 20% on team fit.
